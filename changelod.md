# Changelog

## [0.3.0] - 2023-10-27
### Changed
- **ML Framework:** Migrated from Scikit-Learn to **PyTorch**.
- **Model Architecture:** Switched from Random Forest to **LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory)**.
- **Data Strategy:** Introduced Sliding Window sequences for time-series forecasting.

## [0.2.0] - 2023-10-27
- **Data Source:** Pivoted to NASA PCoE dataset.
